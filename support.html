Carsona for iOS — help & frequently asked questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sandbox Mode?

Sandbox Mode lets you explore Carsona without a real OBD-II adapter. It simulates realistic vehicle data so you can try every feature — gauges, trip logging, AI diagnostics — before connecting to your car.

You can enable Sandbox Mode from Settings → Advanced Settings → Developer. No Bluetooth hardware is required.

Which OBD-II adapters are compatible?

Carsona works with Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) ELM327-compatible adapters. Most adapters sold as "Bluetooth 4.0+" or "BLE OBD2" will work. Common compatible adapters include:

  • OBDLink MX+ (recommended)
  • Vgate iCar Pro BLE
  • Veepeak OBDCheck BLE+

Not compatible: Classic Bluetooth (non-BLE) adapters, Wi-Fi adapters, and USB adapters are not supported. If your adapter pairs through iOS Settings → Bluetooth rather than appearing inside the app, it is likely a classic Bluetooth device and won't work with Carsona.

What are the optional API keys for?

Carsona is usable without cloud API keys. Depending on the build you are using, extra cloud features may be enabled either by entering your own keys in Settings → API Keys or by app-provided defaults in limited development, beta, or review builds:

  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI-powered diagnostic explanations and voice assistant
  • ElevenLabs — Natural-sounding text-to-speech for AI responses

These integrations do not require user API keys:

  • Open-Meteo — Live weather data on your dashboard
  • NHTSA — Vehicle recall and safety lookups by VIN
  • EPA — Fuel economy specs and vehicle data

Keys that you enter are stored locally on your device and are sent directly to each service. Carsona does not currently operate an intermediary cloud service for those requests.

Does Carsona write to my car's computer?

No. Carsona is strictly read-only. It uses OBD-II Mode 01 for live data and Modes 03, 07, and 0A to read diagnostic trouble codes. Only safe ELM327 configuration commands are sent. Mode 04 (clear DTCs) is permanently prohibited. Carsona will never reset monitors, trigger actuator tests, or modify any vehicle settings.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays on your device. Carsona has no cloud backend, no user accounts, and no analytics. Uninstalling the app deletes all data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

The app can't find my adapter

Try these steps:

  • Make sure your adapter is plugged into the OBD-II port and the vehicle ignition is on (at least ACC position).
  • Confirm your adapter is BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy), not classic Bluetooth or Wi-Fi.
  • Check that Bluetooth is enabled in iOS Settings.
  • If the adapter appears in iOS Settings → Bluetooth, forget it there — BLE devices should only connect through the app, not through system pairing.
  • Force-quit Carsona and reopen it, or toggle Bluetooth off and back on.
