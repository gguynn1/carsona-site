Privacy Policy

Carsona for iOS · Bundle ID: com.obd2dashboard.app · Effective March 24, 2026

Overview

Carsona is a local-first OBD-II vehicle dashboard. Your data stays on your device. There are no user accounts, no analytics SDKs, and no data brokers. We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal information.

Data We Access

Carsona requests the following device permissions, each for a specific purpose:

Permission Purpose Stored?
Bluetooth Communicate with your OBD-II adapter to read vehicle diagnostics (read-only — Carsona never writes to your vehicle's ECU) Diagnostic values stored locally on device
Location Provide GPS context for trip logging and fetch local weather conditions Stored locally; coordinates sent to Open-Meteo for weather context when weather features are active (no API key required)
Motion & Fitness Measure G-force and vehicle dynamics for the dashboard display Processed in real time; not persisted beyond the current session
Camera Scan your vehicle's VIN barcode for quick vehicle identification Camera frames are processed on-device; images are not saved or transmitted
Microphone & Speech Recognition Push-to-talk voice commands and an optional foreground-only hands-free wake phrase while driving Audio is processed by Apple's on-device speech recognition; raw audio is not recorded or stored by Carsona

Optional Third-Party Services

Carsona can optionally connect to external APIs if you choose to enable those features. Depending on the build you are using, access may come from API keys you entered in Settings or from app-provided credentials included for limited development, beta, or review use. Carsona does not currently proxy those requests through its own servers; data flows directly from your device to the selected service.

Service What's Sent Their Privacy Policy
Anthropic (Claude AI) Vehicle data context and your prompts for AI-assisted diagnostics anthropic.com/privacy
ElevenLabs (TTS) Text for voice synthesis of AI responses elevenlabs.io/privacy
Open-Meteo GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude) for weather data open-meteo.com/terms
NHTSA VIN for recall and safety lookups nhtsa.gov/privacy-policy
EPA Vehicle year/make/model for fuel economy and specs epa.gov/privacy
Apple MapKit Coordinates for reverse geocoding (location names) apple.com/privacy

Credential handling. API keys that you enter are stored locally on your device. Some development, beta, or review builds may also include app-provided defaults for selected services. Carsona has no server component today, so communication flows directly from your device to the service in use. Disabling a feature stops communication with that service, and removing your saved key removes your local override.

Data Storage & Security

  • All vehicle data, trip logs, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Core Data and UserDefaults.
  • API keys are stored locally on your device in the app's private data directory.
  • Carsona does not operate any backend servers, databases, or cloud storage.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Data Sharing

Carsona does not:

  • Sell or rent your data to anyone
  • Share data with advertising networks
  • Include analytics or tracking SDKs
  • Transmit data to our servers (we have none)
  • Create user profiles or accounts

Children's Privacy

Carsona is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the effective date at the top of the page will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
